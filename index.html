Our Heritage

A Family Tradition of Trust

For over a quarter of a century, AJE Services Ltd has been at the forefront of site investigation and drainage solutions across the South of the UK. Founded and led by Nick and Claire Jones, our company embodies the values of integrity, precision, and personal accountability.

What started as a small family operation has grown into a trusted partner for major developers and residential homeowners alike. Our 25 years of experience means there is very little we haven't seen—and nothing we haven't been able to solve.

We pride ourselves on providing a seamless link between technical data collection and practical, on-site implementation. Every project, no matter the scale, receives the same meticulous attention to detail that defines the AJE name.
